Here is the site plan for the White Water Rafting site

Purpose

Create a site to expand our online presence and drive adventurers to our business. Allow potential adventurers to search and book rafting trips. Include a place for payments and signing online wavers

Audience

Our target audience is adventurous people ages 18-50. We are looking for tourists as well as locals that want a guided white water adventure. We have tours for beginners as well as experienced rafters.
